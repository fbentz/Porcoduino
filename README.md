# Porcoduino
Porcoduino is an experimentation to craft a Zeppelin in Arduino.

## Electronics parts
You needs electronics parts:

* 1 L293D
* 2 Xbee Module
* 1 Arduino
* 2 DC Motors
* 1 XBee shield (for Arduino)
* 1 XBee shield (to connect on your computer)
